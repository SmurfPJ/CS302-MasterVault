Setup Master Password

Your master password is the key to all of your data. It encrypts your information, making sure that only you have access to it.




Avoid common words: Do not use easily guessable passwords like "password" or "123456".

Keep it memorable: You'll need to remember your master password, so choose something that you can recall without writing it down.

Never share your master password: Keep it confidential to ensure your data remains secure.
